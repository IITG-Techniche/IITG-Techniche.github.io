9 APRIL 2023

The Guwahati Half Marathon (GHM) one of the oldest and largest events of North East India organised by students of IIT Guwahati and is the largest student organised marathon in India.

This year, Techniche aims to organize a more mammoth marathon than the last one. We come up with the theme for GHM 2023 - "Run for Zero Hunger." This endeavor aims to generate awareness and shed light on the down-and-out. The only motto is to provide a healthy lifestyle for one and all.

“The fight against hunger is the fight for a better world .”
Ban Ki-Moon

Categories

21 Km
Glory Run
This is a 21 kilometre half marathon, the first of its kind in North-East India and the flagship event of the day. The race is open to everyone above the age of 14.
6 Km
Spirit Run
This is a 6 km run specifically designed for the citizens of Guwahati. The run is open to participants of all ages.
